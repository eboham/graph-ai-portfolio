About the Course

The programme distills generative‑AI best practices into seven bite‑sized modules:

  • Documents, Presentations & Websites – create polished content and pages with LLMs.
  • Automated Data Analysis – clean, visualise and report data using Code Interpreter, Julius & Windsurf.
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis – literature review, summarisation and sentiment tools like Elicit & LiteRev.
  • Custom GPTs & Chatbots – build task‑specific assistants with RAG systems and Botpress workflows.
  • AI Workflows for Task Automation – orchestrate Lindy, Make.com and GSheets to reclaim hours.
  • GenAI for Communication & Outreach – craft visuals, videos and marketing assets with AI.
  • Capstone Project & Showcase – design and present a real‑world AI solution.
