About Me

I am a Ghanaian molecular medicine researcher with close to a decade of clinical‑research experience. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD at the University of Sapienza while collaborating on Plasmodium falciparum gametocyte and bone‑marrow micro‑environment research in partnership with the LITD Lab at KNUST and the Alano Lab (ISS).

I previously served as a Research Assistant at the Kumasi Centre for Collaborative Research in Tropical Medicine, contributing to multicountry studies on SARS‑CoV‑2, dengue and meningitis.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is a hands‑on programme covering practical AI tools and workflows. The curriculum spans content creation, automated data analysis, literature review, chatbot development, workflow automation, communication assets and a culminating capstone project.

Documents, Presentations & Websites

  • Generate high‑quality text and web content.
  • LLM fundamentals, Markdown, no‑code sites.

Automated Data Analysis

  • AI‑powered data cleaning, visualization & reporting.
  • Tools: ChatGPT Code Interpreter, Julius, Windsurf.

GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

  • Literature search, summarization & sentiment analysis.
  • Tools: Elicit, LiteRev, custom LLM pipelines.

Custom GPTs & Chatbots

  • RAG systems, Botpress workflows, API integrations.

AI Workflows for Task Automation

  • Lindy and Make.com for email & admin task automation.

GenAI for Communication & Outreach

  • Create visual & multimedia assets for public engagement.

Capstone Project & Showcase

  • Design and pitch a real‑world AI solution.
